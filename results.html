StaticMaps

For Static maps, we have created induvidual maps for every factors considered and the final walkability index too. Apart from it, we have created a mapbook with a few layers overlayed on the final walkability index to have a better understanding of the walkability with the context to it's neighbourhood.

Induvidual Maps

Each factor is mapped with it's final ratio with the relavant layer overlayed to give a better understanding of the output. These maps have the formula used in raster calculator to give the context on how it was processed, with it's share of weight in the final index. In terms of the final walkability map, each factor with its share of weights for the index is given to explain how it was arrived. Added to it, some infographics with the census data make it more informative and gives a better understanding about the walkability.

Mapbook

With all the factors discussed, we wanted to overlay few layers over the final walkability index map to get a better understanding with respect to the neighbourhood. This mapbook splits the city of Peterbourough into 52 parts in grids and displaying all the features in detail.
